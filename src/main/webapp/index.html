About Swap

Swap provides an easy-to-use platform for college students to easily swap or give away items. Users are able to access a public feed, where they can view posts from other users on the items they want to swap. Our users

Swap is primarily geared towards college students who are looking to get rid of old items (and replace them with new ones). It's meant to be a central location for students to easily find items they need and easily get rid of items they don't need.
